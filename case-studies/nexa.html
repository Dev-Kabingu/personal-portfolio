Fintech UX Case Study

Nexa Banking App

Designing a modern mobile banking experience focused on simplicity, accessibility, trust, and frictionless digital finance for mobile-first users.

UX Research Fintech Mobile UI Product Design

Role

UI/UX Designer

Timeline

4 Weeks

Tools

Figma

Platform

Mobile Banking

Problem Statement

Traditional banking apps feel cluttered, outdated, and overwhelming.

Most banking apps prioritize functionality over usability, resulting in complicated navigation, overwhelming dashboards, poor onboarding experiences, and too many steps for simple actions. Nexa was designed to rethink the mobile banking experience through cleaner interactions, intuitive navigation, and a premium modern fintech interface.

UX Research

Understanding frustrations in digital finance.

Research focused on analyzing usability gaps in existing banking applications including onboarding, money transfers, transaction tracking, and dashboard experiences.

Confusing dashboard hierarchy
Too many steps for transfers
Lack of spending insights
Weak visual hierarchy
Design Process

From research insights to polished banking experiences.

01

03

Authentication Flow

Login & Sign Up Screens

The onboarding experience was designed to feel secure, intuitive and frictionless while guiding users through account creation and identity verification.

Dashboard Experience

Smart banking dashboard built for clarity.

The dashboard experience focuses on fast access to key actions, spending insights, transaction visibility and simplified financial management.

Final Solution

A clean, intuitive and modern mobile banking experience.

Smart Dashboard

Simplified financial visibility with spending insights, quick transfers and personalized actions.

Faster Transfers

Reduced friction in sending money through cleaner workflows and fewer interaction steps.

Reflection

Key takeaways from designing Nexa.

This project strengthened my understanding of fintech UX, accessibility, and user-centered product thinking. I learned how simplifying interactions and improving information hierarchy can dramatically improve usability while still maintaining a visually premium experience.
