About Me

A Passionate Developer from Kenya

Hi, I'm Mercy Kabingu, a UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer from Kenya. I have a strong foundation in web development and UI/UX design.

As a UI/UX Designer, I focus on creating intuitive, user-friendly interfaces that enhance user experience. I specialize in wireframing, prototyping, and user flow optimization. My approach is user-centered, ensuring that the design speaks directly to the user’s needs.

I currently completed my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering at Kirinyaga University, where I’ve gained a deep understanding of software principles and development practices.

I also specialized in the MERN stack through the Power Learn Project Africa & Safaricom Hook training programs, sharpening my skills in MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js.

I'm passionate about creating intuitive, user-friendly designs and continuously learning to stay ahead in the ever-evolving tech space.
