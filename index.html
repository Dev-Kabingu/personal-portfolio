Who I Am

I’m Mercy Kabingu, a Product Designer & UI/UX Specialist passionate about crafting digital experiences that blend creativity with functionality. With a background in software engineering and design, I bring a unique perspective to building interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and user‑focused.

My work is inspired by minimal aesthetics, authentic branding, and the belief that design should solve problems while telling a story. I thrive on collaboration, innovation, and turning ideas into impactful products.
